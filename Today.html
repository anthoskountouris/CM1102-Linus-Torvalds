Linux

Linus Torvalds creation, Linux is still relevant today and is popular in many specific areas. It’s a fact that almost 60% of all websites on the internet run on Linux at the moment and it’s used both to run parts of the internet, as well as to run networks in offices and homes.

Also because of its stability, security and flexibility, Linux is the best choice for storing huge databases of information. Furthermore, many of Linux systems can connect and work together on a single task e.g. weather forecasting, physics simulations, computer graphics rendering, etc.
