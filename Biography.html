Linus Torvalds is a Computer Scientist and programmer who is known for his development of the Linux operating system. He was born in Helsinki, Finland in December 28,1969. His parents are Nils and Anna Torvalds and his wife Tove Torvalds with whom he has 3 children.

His interest in computers began when he was 10 years old when he got his grandfather’s computer. He purchased his first computer in 1988 while he was a Computer Science student but he was not satisfied with the computer’s operating system.

So, he decided to build his own PC-based version of Unix, the Linux. The first version of Linux was released by 1994 after the help of many programmers in retooling and refining the software.
